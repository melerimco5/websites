Horizons of Light is a sanctuary for seekers ready to move beyond the limits of the physical world and elevate their consciousness. Curated by Life & Career Coach and Spiritual Leader, Jaime Melendez, this space blends timeless wisdom with practical tools to help you align body, mind, and spirit and rediscover meaning in your next chapter.

Why Horizons of Light

  • Awareness: See your life with compassion and truth.
  • Alignment: Choose actions that honor your values.
  • Presence: Tend to body, mind, and spirit with care.
  • Purpose: Live from your essence, not expectations.

After 20+ years in the U.S., Jaime returned to Chile to reconnect with his roots, care for his parents, and step fully into a path of mysticism—studying Kabbalah at the Kabbalah Centre in Los Angeles (since 2013; trainings with Michael Berg and family), completing the Miller Introduction to Judaism Program at American Jewish University, and earning certificates in Kabbalah, Astrology, and Spiritual Channeling.

About Jaime Melendez

I guide adults 40+ through meaningful life transitions by uniting spiritual wisdom with pragmatic coaching. Former Trade Commissioner of Chile (ProChile, Chicago) and award-winning entrepreneur (RIMCO Foods, MBDA "Corporation of the Year," 2008), I now focus on helping others align purpose, values, and action.
